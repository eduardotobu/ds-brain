## Narrative

Machine learning is fundamentally about building systems that learn representations and rules from data, rather than being explicitly programmed with them. This vault tracks my journey from understanding how a simple linear regression draws a line through data points, all the way up to how massive transformer networks synthesize human language. By mapping out classical algorithms before deep learning, and keeping a firm grasp on the underlying linear algebra and calculus, the "magic" of AI becomes a structured, understandable engineering discipline.
